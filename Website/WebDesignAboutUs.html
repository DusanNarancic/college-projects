ABOUT US

Dred Company over fifteen years of experience in importing and selling used cars and trucks. In our salon we offer a wide selection of vehicles of new generation of renowned manufacturers.Used cars from our carefully selected taking into account the wishes and needs of our customers.Our suppliers are well known and positioned leasing companies from the European Union. Our job and mission is to provide our customers with quality and reliable vehicleat the best price. Check out why our offer cars apart from others. Our technical inspection offers the possibility of payment of all fees and collecting the necessary documents for the registration of vehicles in any registration area. We issue temporary license plates to transport the car to the destination. Our service provides quality and affordable repair services for all makes and types of vehicles with accompanying guarantees.
